This Privacy Policy describes how Nestoric Digital LLP ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Nexify mobile application ("App"). By using Nexify, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Last updated: April 24, 2026  ·  Effective date: April 24, 2026

01.Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the Nexify service:

Account & Identity

Full name Email address Phone number Profile photo User role (client / worker / admin)

Service Activity

Service requests created Request status & history Chat messages Uploaded files & documents

Payment Information

Transaction ID Payment status Amount & tier

Note: Full card/bank details are processed directly by Razorpay. We do not store sensitive payment credentials.

Device & Technical

FCM device token Device type & OS version App version

02.How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Nexify account
  • To process service requests between clients and workers
  • To facilitate in-app chat between users
  • To process payments securely through Razorpay
  • To send push notifications about your requests and activity
  • To enable file and document uploads via Cloudinary
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To improve app performance and fix issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

03.Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted service providers necessary to operate Nexify:

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, push notifications (FCM), real-time database
  • Razorpay — Payment processing. Subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary — File and image storage
  • Render — Backend server hosting

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

04.Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and token-based authentication (JWT) for API access.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your login credentials confidential.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

05.Permissions We Request

Nexify requests the following device permissions:

Permission Details

  • Camera — To capture and upload photos for service requests and profile pictures
  • Storage / Media — To select and upload files and images from your device
  • Internet — Required to connect to Nexify servers
  • Post Notifications — To receive push notifications about your requests and messages (Android 13+)
  • Network State — To detect connectivity and handle offline scenarios gracefully

All permissions are requested only when needed and used solely for the purposes described above.

06.Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdrawal of consent — Disable notifications or revoke camera/storage permissions at any time via your device settings
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

07.Children's Privacy

Nexify is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

08.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Nexify after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
